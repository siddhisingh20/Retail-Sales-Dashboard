# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Retail Sales Performance Dashboard is an end-to-end data analytics project that analyzes retail sales data to identify business trends, customer behavior, product performance, and regional sales insights.

This project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow, including data cleaning, SQL analysis, and interactive dashboard creation using Tableau Public.

## 🚀 Project Objectives

- Analyze monthly sales trends
- Identify the most profitable products
- Compare sales and profit across regions
- Analyze category-wise performance
- Identify top customers by sales
- Evaluate average discount by category

## 📂 Project Workflow

### 1. Data Collection
- Downloaded the Sample Superstore dataset.

### 2. Data Cleaning (Excel)
- Checked for missing values.
- Removed duplicate records.
- Formatted date columns.
- Created helper columns for Month and Year.

### 3. SQL Analysis
Performed business analysis using SQL queries:
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Top 10 Products by Profit
- Region-wise Sales & Profit
- Category-wise Sales & Profit
- Top 10 Customers
- Average Discount by Category

### 4. Dashboard Development
Built an interactive Tableau dashboard containing:
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Top 10 Products by Profit
- Region-wise Sales & Profit
- Category-wise Sales & Profit
- Top 10 Customers
- Average Discount by Category

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Technology products generated the highest profits.
- Sales performance varied significantly across regions.
- A small group of customers contributed a large share of total sales.
- Higher discounts did not always result in higher profitability.
- Category-wise analysis highlighted differences in sales and profit.
